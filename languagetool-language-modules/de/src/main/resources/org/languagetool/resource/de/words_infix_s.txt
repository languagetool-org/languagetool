# Non-compound nouns that require an infix 's' when another noun is appended
# to form a compound noun.
# For now, several conditions need to be met to make this work:
#  * The compound must be splittable by our compound splitter. Use https://community.languagetool.org/germanCompoundSplit/
#    to check if that's the case. If not, add the word part(s) to `ExtendedGermanWordSplitter.extendedList`
#    in `GermanCompoundTokenizer.java`.
#  * The compound must have two parts, not more.
#  * The compound parts must be 3 characters or longer.
#  * If a compound still isn't accepted, make sure its parts are accepted by the speller and
#    that they have a POS tag.
# Used in GermanSpellerRule.ignorePotentiallyMisspelledWord().
